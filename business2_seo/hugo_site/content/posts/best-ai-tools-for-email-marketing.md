## How to Choose the Right AI Email Marketing Tool

Start by identifying your primary use case: Are you building sales funnels (GetResponse), managing e-commerce revenue (Klaviyo), or optimizing existing campaigns with AI copy (Writesonic)?

**Next, evaluate these factors:**

1. **Integration needs** — Does the tool connect to your CRM, e-commerce platform, or existing tech stack?
2. **Team size** — Beginners benefit from simpler interfaces (Brevo, HubSpot), while agencies scale with powerful automation (GetResponse, Klaviyo)
3. **Budget constraints** — Free tiers vary significantly; test before upgrading
4. **AI priorities** — If copywriting is your bottleneck, prioritize AI generation features; if segmentation matters most, choose platforms with behavioral automation
5. **Feature completeness** — All-in-one platforms (GetResponse) save time; specialized tools (Writesonic) excel in narrow areas

Test at least two platforms with your actual email list before committing to annual billing. Most offer 30-day free trials that reveal limitations specific to your workflow.
