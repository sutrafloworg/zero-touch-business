Frequently Asked Questions

Which cities and business categories do you monitor?
We currently monitor 5 major US cities — New York, Los Angeles, Austin, Miami, and Denver — across 5 high-value business categories: personal injury lawyers, emergency plumbers, cosmetic dentists, luxury real estate agents, and regenerative medicine clinics. We're expanding coverage based on demand.
How do I know if my business was detected?
If your business appears in Google Maps results for any of our tracked categories and cities, and your ranking drops, you'll receive a free alert email automatically. No signup or account required — Search Sentinel finds you and reaches out directly.
What if my ranking is stable?
Weekly Monitoring subscribers receive a brief "all clear" confirmation email every Monday, confirming your position held steady. You'll always know your status — whether your rank dropped or stayed the same.
How fast will I receive my report after paying?
Reports are generated by AI and emailed automatically within minutes of payment confirmation. There's no manual queue — your full PDF is built and sent the moment your Stripe payment clears.
Does Stripe verify the payment amount? What if someone pays less?
Yes — Stripe Payment Links have a fixed price set in the dashboard. Customers cannot modify the amount. The checkout enforces the price, and your report is only delivered after a successful payment for the correct amount is confirmed by Stripe's webhook.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, anytime. Simply reply "cancel" to any monitoring email, or manage your subscription directly through Stripe's customer portal. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no minimum terms.
Is my business data private?
We analyze only publicly available Google Maps data — the same information any user can see. We never access your Google Business Profile account, and we don't require any credentials or logins. Your email is used only for report delivery.
